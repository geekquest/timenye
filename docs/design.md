# Timenye

## Background 

Timenye brings together athletes in different sports for social games, competitions and activitiies.


Basically the target audience for timenye are people who

- Play Social sports including: football, volleyball, netball
- Go to the Gym

## Features

- List Teams
- View Team Sponsors
- Team Merch Shop
- Arrange Games
- View Player Stats
- View Team Pages
